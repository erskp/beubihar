Bihar Engineering University

This [Unofficial] Website is made for providing Study Materials to Bihar Engineering Students.

Established in 2021, Bihar Engineering University (BEU) is a modern institution that governs technical education in Bihar. Created by the state government, its core mission is to affiliate and unify all engineering branches, fostering academic excellence and innovation on a statewide scale.

As the single governing body, BEU guides the academic framework for thousands of students across dozens of institutions. The university is dedicated to developing skilled engineers and thought leaders prepared for a dynamic world, aiming to elevate the quality of technical education and drive technological advancement throughout the region.
